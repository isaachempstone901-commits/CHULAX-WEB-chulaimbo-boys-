Welcome to our page

Chulaimbo Boys High School (also called Chulaimbo Boys Secondary School) is a public, all-boys boarding secondary school located in Kisumu West District, Maseno Division, along Kisumu–Busia Road — about 8 km from Maseno Township and roughly 20 km from Kisumu City. It falls under the Kenyan Ministry of Education and follows the national curriculum.

Chulaimbo Boys High School is a premier public boarding institution in Maseno South, Kisumu.
Known as a "Center of Excellence," it delivers holistic education through diverse STEM, Social Science, and Arts pathways.
The school boasts a rich history, strong academic performance, and vibrant extracurricular activities, fostering a nurturing environment for student growth and success.

The school is known by the name "CHULAX"
