The school provides modern classrooms, science labs, a library, boarding facilities, and sports programs. Chulaimbo Boys High School is a well‑known public boys’ secondary school in Kisumu County, Kenya. It is categorized as an Extra County school under the Kenya national education system, meaning it draws students from a wider region thanks to its strong academic reputation. The school offers a full boarding programme where students live and study on campus, and it’s registered with the Kenya National Examinations Council (KNEC) under the code 39737022.Geographically, Chulaimbo Boys High School is situated in the Kisumu West District within the Maseno Division, along the Kisumu–Busia Road. It lies roughly 8 km from Maseno township and about 20km from Kisumu City, making it accessible by road from the regional centre of Kisumu. This location places the school in a semi-rural educational hub near the town of Chulaimbo in western Kenya. The surrounding area of Chulaimbo, where the school is based, is part of Kisumu West and functions as both a residential and commercial centre in Nyanza Region. Chulaimbo itself lies along the busy Kisumu-Busia corridor, with local institutions, health facilities, and businesses supporting the community’s development.The school’s position here gives students access to both town amenities and the quieter environment conducive to study.
